# About Me

## Who I Am
I'm a computer science student and aspiring software engineer with a deep passion for building systems that sit at the intersection of machine learning, security, and practical software engineering. I believe the best technology solves real problems elegantly, and I'm driven by the challenge of turning cutting-edge research into tools people can actually use.

## What Drives Me
My work spans three main areas that I find endlessly fascinating:

**Machine Learning & AI**: I'm particularly interested in how we can make ML models more robust and adaptable. My current research on domain adaptation with Mixture of Experts architectures is exploring how models can gracefully handle real-world variability — something that's critical for deploying AI systems that work reliably outside the lab.

**Blockchain & Cryptography**: Through Pangochain, I've explored how cryptographic primitives can be combined in novel ways to build secure, efficient distributed systems. I'm fascinated by the mathematical foundations of trust in digital systems.

**Full-Stack Engineering**: I enjoy the craft of building complete, polished applications — from designing clean APIs to creating intuitive user interfaces. There's a unique satisfaction in deploying a system that real users interact with.

## My Approach
I believe in:
- **Learning by building**: Every concept I study, I implement. Theory without practice is incomplete.
- **Open source**: Sharing knowledge and code makes everyone better. All my major projects are open-sourced.
- **Clean code**: Readable, well-tested, well-documented code is non-negotiable.
- **Continuous learning**: The field moves fast, and I'm always exploring new techniques, papers, and tools.

## What I'm Looking For
I'm seeking opportunities where I can contribute to meaningful engineering challenges — whether that's building ML infrastructure, developing secure systems, or creating developer tools. I thrive in environments where I can learn from experienced engineers while bringing my own energy and fresh perspectives to the team.
